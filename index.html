Crocodile animals are amazing reptiles!

Special Features! 🐊

They look very special! Here is what makes them unique:

  • 🐊 Body & Legs: They have long bodies and short legs.
  • 🐟 Strong Tail: They have powerful tails for fast swimming.
  • 🛡️ Scaly Skin: They have bumpy, protective scaly skin.
  • 🦷 Sharp Teeth: They have many sharp teeth for eating.
👀 Their eyes and nose are near the top of their head. So they can stay low in the water, but still see and breathe!

Protect Their Homes! 🌿

Crocodile animals love rivers, lakes, and wetlands. The river is their beautiful home!

But trash in the water makes it dirty and dangerous. We must protect them by:

  • 🚯 No Littering: We should not throw trash in the water.
  • 🧼 Keep Waters Clean: We should keep our rivers sparkling and clean.
  • 🌳 Save Nature: We should protect trees and plants near the river.
🐠 Clean rivers help fish, birds, plants, and crocodiles live happily together!
